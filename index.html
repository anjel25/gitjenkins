Welcome to the Vishnu Purana!
This Hindu scripture, one of the eighteen major Puranas,
primarily focuses on the god Vishnu and his avatars, including Rama and Krishna, and explores cosmology, genealogy, and legends.


Here's a more detailed overview:Content:
The Vishnu Purana covers a wide range of topics, including:
Cosmogony and Cosmology:It delves into the creation and structure of the universe.
Mythical Genealogy: It narrates the lineages of gods, sages, and kings. Legends and Stories:
It recounts various legends and stories, particularly those related to Vishnu and his avatars.
